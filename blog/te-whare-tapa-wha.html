TE WHARE TAPA WHĀ - MY WELLBEING PLAN

Taha Tiana (Physical Wellbeing)

  • - Going for a run in the morning before class 3x a week
  • - Stretching on lunchtime breaks everyday
  • - Go to the gym in the evening 4x a week after class

Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing)

  • - Call my family in the weekend, and at least once during the week.

Taha Hinengaro (Emtional and Mental Wellbeing)

  • - Practice a small mediation before class each day
  • - Practice mindfulness at lunchtime each day during the week
  • - Listen to guided meditations before going to sleep

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

  • - Volunteer/ask to do something for someone else once a day

Whenua (Interconnections to lead to the land and environment)

  • - Walk down to the beach and go a for a swim once a week
  • - In the weekends walk an exisiting or new trial
